## FAQs

**1. How does SpendLens calculate savings?**
We use official published pricing from each vendor's pricing page, verified and dated. We check three things for every tool: whether you're on the right plan for your seat count, whether a cheaper plan from the same vendor would cover your needs, and whether a competing tool is meaningfully cheaper for your primary use case. Every recommendation links to the source pricing.

**2. Do I need to connect any accounts or share payment details?**
No. You enter your tools, plans, seats, and monthly spend manually. SpendLens never asks for API keys, billing access, or login credentials to any AI tool. It's a calculator, not an integration.

**3. Is this actually free?**
Yes. Running an audit costs nothing and requires no account. We ask for your email after the audit if you want to receive the results — that's optional.

**4. How often should I run an audit?**
AI tool pricing changes frequently — vendors add plans, adjust tiers, and run promotions. Running an audit every 1–2 months takes 2 minutes and keeps your spend current. We'll notify you if we detect a pricing change that affects your setup.

**5. What happens if my potential savings are over $500/month?**
For teams with significant waste, we surface a referral to Credex, a procurement platform that can negotiate enterprise AI contracts on your behalf. This is optional — your audit results and recommendations are shown regardless.
